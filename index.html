About Me

Hi, I'm Akshatha,a Data Analyst by profession and an artist and teacher by passion. I've been conducting classes for more than a year and love helping others find joy in art. Along with visual art, I have a deep passion for Carnatic music, which I’ve been learning and teaching with dedication. Music and art together help me connect more deeply with people and culture.

Carnatic Music Classes

I also conduct Junior Grade Carnatic music classes for all age groups.

  • Vocal and theory training
  • Weekly sessions (online/offline)
  • Focus on shruti, laya, and compositions
  • Tailored curriculum based on student level

If you're interested in learning traditional South Indian classical music in a warm, encouraging environment — you're in the right place!
